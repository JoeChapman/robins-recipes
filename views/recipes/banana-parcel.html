Banana and Date Parcel

Ingredients

  • puff pastry
  • 2 tbsp caster surgar
  • 2 bananas
  • 10 dates
  • 1 egg

Method

  1. Turn the oven to 180Â°C
  2. Put down the baking tray and lay greese proof paper over it.
  3. Roll out the puff pastry
  4. Put the puff pastry on the tray.
  5. Cut 1 banana and lay it over half the pastry
  6. Cut the dates into pieces and lay them on top of the banana
  7. Sprinkle 1 tbsp of caster sugar over the banana and dates
  8. Fold the pastry over and pinch the edges together
  9. Slice the other banana and lay the pieces on top of the pastry
  10. Sprinkle the remaining tbsn of sugar over the banana slices
  11. Brush the top of the pastry and banana slices with egg
  12. Put in the oven for 15 mins or until golden brown
