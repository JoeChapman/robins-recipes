Shortbread Heart cake

Ingredients

  • Shortbread

    • Butter
    • sugar
    • flour
    • poping candy

  • Icing

    • Icing sugar
    • Pink food colouring
    • water
    • Sprinkles

Method

Shortbread

  1. Turn the oven on to 180Â°C
  2. Mix the flour ,sugar and popping candy in a large bowl
  3. Rub the butter into the flour and sugar
  4. Beat it together till a smooth paste
  5. put in the oven for 15 min to cook
  6. When golden brown, take out and cut into heart shapes
  7. Leave to cool while you make the icing

Icing

  1. Mix the water and icing sugar together.
  2. Add the food coloringto the water and icing sugar
  3. Mix till all the icing is the same color as the food coloring
  4. Poor the icing on the shortbread and add the sprinkles
  5. Leave to set, then eat!
